Kingdom of heaven (2005)

Film follows Balian, a blacksmith who becomes a knight after the death of his wife and child. He joins the Crusaders in Jerusalem, where he must navigate political and religious conflict while defending the city against the Muslim leader Saladin. Balian rises to a position of leadership, balancing his quest for honor with his struggle for peace between Christian and Muslim forces. The film explores themes of faith, loyalty, and the complexities of war.

The Gentlemen (2019)

The film follows Mickey Pearson, an American expat who has built a successful marijuana empire in London. When he decides to sell his business, various criminal elements, including rival gangs and opportunistic figures, plot to take control of his empire. As tensions rise, Mickey and his associates must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and violence. The film is marked by sharp dialogue, witty characters, and twists, all set against the backdrop of the British underworld.
